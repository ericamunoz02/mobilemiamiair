Don’t let our name fool you

While we first spread our wings over Miami in 1991, today you will see our aircraft around the globe. Miami Air International is the perfect choice when you need “your own aircraft” to move from 68 to 168 people between virtually any two points on the planet.

What makes us premier?

All we do is charter. And we do a lot! Our business is built to only serve groups like yours, and you are our number one priority. In addition to concentrating our efforts on being the best in those things you want from any airline – like industry leading safety-programs and 23 years of proven reliability and financial stability – we provide a suite of services unparalleled by any other airline.
